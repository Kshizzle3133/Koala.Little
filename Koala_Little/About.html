About Us...

We focus on the education and protect of the beautiful creatures known as koalas

Here are some facts you may not have known

  • Koalas do not live in social groups.
  • Koalas sleep up to 18 hours a day (thats about 6 hours of activity per day)
  • and the scariest fact of all is that their wildlife population numbers are dwindiling
